Initial Planning Document

Purpose Statement

The purpose of this site is to provide basic information about what the weather is like at the moment, what it has been like, and what it may be like in the future at a glance so that people can plan their outdoor activities, transportation, and clothing decisions accordingly. People should be able to find out ahead of time whether they will need a jacket, an umbrella, a windshield scrapper, snow chains, a different way to get to their destination, or a different flight.

Target Audience

The demographics of this site could be almost anyone. It could be someone who is getting dressing early in the morning and wants to quickly see what the weather might be like that day so that they dress appropriately. It could be someone who is trying to schedule an outdoor activity with their friends or family at a park or beach and wants to know when the weather will be nice enough for that activity.

This site meets the needs of it visitors by providing at a glance information about what the weather is like outside and what it will be like, while providing detailed information about the weather for someone who would like to know more.

The content is organized so that the most important information is the most prominent and can be consumed with a glance. This site makes the mobile experience less painful by providing the most important information without any participation from the audience, while still allowing them to interact with the site to gain more information.

Peer Review

Samantha Sullivan reviewed my document and said that the organization looked okay, but there was no way to tell what any of the elements were for or what information was being presented on my wireframe sketches. I changed all of my sketches to include more details also realized that I wasn't using the space of the bigger screens, so I rearranged some of the elements and added some more information that wouldn't fit on the smaller screens.
